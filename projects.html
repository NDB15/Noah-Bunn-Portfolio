Earworm (in-progress)

I am building an app that will allow users to see what music others in their area are listening to. Users can connect accounts from Apple Music, Spotify, SoundCloud, or Musi to share their listening history. You can view your own listening data, but you can also automatically exchange profiles with nearby users. When someone with the app is within ~8 meters, their avatar appears on your screen, and you can view their profile and activity. A discovery screen shows all nearby users' profiles in real time, and even if the app is closed, a history list logs everyone you've passed.

Skills Used: React • Vite • TypeScript • Capacitor • GitHub

Small But Mighty

Backend Software Engineer

Small But Mighty is a mobile and web platform empowering communities to discover and support their local businesses. Born from our team of four's firsthand experience as small business owners and employees, we recognized the need to cut through the noise of mainstream retailers and create meaningful connections between consumers and local merchants.

The platform serves as a curated gateway to hidden gems in your neighborhood, eliminating the frustration of endless scrolling through corporate chains. Our mission is to make local shopping and dining not just a choice, but a delightful, intuitive experience.
