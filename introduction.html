Personal Projects

This is one of the projects I've worked on recently: I am building an app that will allow users to see what music others in their area are listening to. Users can connect accounts from Apple Music, Spotify, SoundCloud, or Musi to share their listening history. You can view your own listening data, but you can also automatically exchange profiles with nearby users. When someone with the app is within ~8 meters, their avatar appears on your screen, and you can view their profile and activity. A discovery screen shows all nearby users’ profiles in real time, and even if the app is closed, a history list logs everyone you’ve passed.
